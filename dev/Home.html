MAP :

The Map is designed to provide a more interactive and intuitive way to navigate the website. Instead of using traditional menus, this section allows users to explore different parts of the site through a visually structured layout. In this section, keywords are positioned like locations on a map, each representing a different page of the website. By clicking on a keyword, users are instantly directed to the corresponding section, making the browsing experience seamless and efficient. This approach enhances accessibility, reduces the need for excessive scrolling, and provides a more engaging way to interact with the website. Whether you're exploring or looking for something specific, the Map section ensures you can reach your destination with ease.
